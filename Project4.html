GST Module Implementation at IDFC

  • Requirement analysis & impact study: Assessed regulatory GST guidelines and mapped their impact on existing financial systems, reports, and workflows.
  • System design & configuration: Designed the GST module structure (tax codes, slabs, GL mappings) and configured it within the core application.
  • Data mapping & migration: Identified affected data fields, updated master data, and ensured correct mapping for GST-compliant transactions.
  • Integration with related systems: Coordinated changes with billing, accounting, and reporting applications to maintain end-to-end consistency.
  • Testing & validation: Prepared test scenarios, executed UAT with business teams, and validated calculations, postings, and reports.
  • User training & documentation: Created user guides, conducted training sessions, and supported teams during the transition to GST.
  • Go-live & post-implementation support: Monitored live transactions, resolved issues in real time, and fine-tuned configurations based on feedback.
